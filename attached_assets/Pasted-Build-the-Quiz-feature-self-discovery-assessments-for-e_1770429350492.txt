- "Question 2 of 8"

- Answer options (multiple choice cards)
- Tapping an option selects it (visual feedback)

- Back button to previous question

### Answer Options Format:
- 3-5 options per question
- Each option is a tappable card
- Selected state: colored border, subtle background
