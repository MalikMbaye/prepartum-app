Build a milestone calendar screen at app/milestones.tsx.

This screen shows all pregnancy milestones as a scrollable list
grouped by trimester. It is accessed from the home dashboard
(there should already be a milestones link or card there).

Data: Seed 15 milestones into the existing milestones table
(shared/schema.ts). Each milestone needs:
- title (e.g. "First Heartbeat", "Anatomy Scan", "Baby Shower")
- week_number (which pregnancy week it typically occurs)
- trimester (1, 2, or 3)
- description (1-2 sentences about why it matters)
- icon (an emoji)
- is_completed (boolean, per user — stored in a userMilestones table)

The 15 milestones to seed:
1. Week 6 — First Heartbeat 💓
2. Week 8 — First OB Appointment 🩺
3. Week 10 — First Trimester Screening 📋
4. Week 12 — End of First Trimester 🌱
5. Week 14 — Baby's Sex Detectable 👶
6. Week 16 — First Movements (Quickening) 🦋
7. Week 18 — Anatomy Scan 🔬
8. Week 20 — Halfway There 🎯
9. Week 24 — Viability Milestone 🌟
10. Week 27 — End of Second Trimester ✨
11. Week 28 — Third Trimester Begins 🌙
12. Week 32 — Hospital Tour 🏥
13. Week 35 — Group B Strep Test 💊
14. Week 37 — Full Term 🎊
15. Week 40 — Due Date 🍼

UI:
- Scrollable list grouped by trimester with trimester headers
- Each milestone is a card showing: emoji, title, week number,
  short description, and a checkbox to mark complete
- Completed milestones show a soft checkmark and slightly muted style
- Current week's upcoming milestone is highlighted with a
  "Coming Up" badge in Soft Pink (#F5D6D6)
- Past milestones that weren't marked complete show as faded

- Trimester headers: serif font, 20px

Navigation:
