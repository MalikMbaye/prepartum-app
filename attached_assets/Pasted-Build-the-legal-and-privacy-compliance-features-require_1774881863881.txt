2. PRIVACY POLICY SCREEN at app/privacy-policy.tsx

A scrollable screen with the following content:

Title: Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 30, 2026

Sections:
- What we collect: Name, email, due date, intake responses, 
  reflection answers, photos, voice recordings, user ID
- Why we collect it: To personalize your experience and 
  power app features
- Who we share it with: Supabase (database storage), 
  Anthropic (AI roleplay feature only), Apple/Google 
  (app distribution). We never sell your data.
- How long we keep it: Until you delete your account
- Your rights: You can delete all your data at any time 
  from Settings
- Contact: privacy@prepartumapp.com
- This app is a wellness tool, not a medical app. It does 
  not provide medical advice.

Style: Luminous Editorial (#FFF8F5 background, #5D5066 text,
serif headings, generous padding, scrollable)

4. TERMS ACCEPTANCE in onboarding

On the LAST step of onboarding (before the celebration 
screen), add:

- A checkbox (default unchecked, required to proceed)
- Label: "I agree to the Privacy Policy and Terms of Service"
- "Privacy Policy" is a tappable link opening 
  app/privacy-policy.tsx
- "Terms of Service" is a tappable link opening 
  app/terms-of-service.tsx
- The "Continue" or "Get Started" button is DISABLED 
  until the checkbox is ticked
- When accepted, save to users table:
  acceptedTermsAt: current timestamp
  acceptedTermsVersion: "1.0"

Add these two columns to the users table in 
shared/schema.ts:
  acceptedTermsAt: timestamp
  acceptedTermsVersion: text

Run SQL migration:
ALTER TABLE users ADD COLUMN IF NOT EXISTS 
  accepted_terms_at timestamp;
ALTER TABLE users ADD COLUMN IF NOT EXISTS 
  accepted_terms_version text;
